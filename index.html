Learn About NFTs

An NFT, or non-fungible token, is a unique digital asset that represents ownership of a specific item, such as art, music, or videos, stored on a blockchain.

To create an NFT, choose a blockchain, mint the asset, upload your digital file, and set the metadata and price on an NFT marketplace.
